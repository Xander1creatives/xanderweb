MIKA HOTELS

Mika Hotels - Kabulonga, chamba valley, MCC and jesmondine is a cozy boutique hotel ideal for business travelers, tourists and families.

MIKA HOTEL-KABULONGA

Located in the heart of Lusaka’s Kabulonga residential area, Mika Hotel is 25Km from KK international airport and about 10km from the central business district.

MIKA HOTEL-JESMONDINE

Situated in the residential area of Jesmondine on the corner of First and Central Street. Mika Lodge is about 10Km away from the Central Business district and 15KM from the Kenneth Kaunda International Airport. The lodge is in proximity to notable sites like: The university of Zambia, The Mulungushi International Conference Center, The Chainama Golf Club and some renowned shopping malls in town.

MIKA CONVENTION CENTER

Mika Convention Center is located about 10KM from the Kenneth Kaunda international Airport in the outskates of Chongwe district that boarders Lusaka city. The property has superb events facilities with in-door venues of between 10 to 800 seating capacities and an amphitheater of 10,000 seating capacity.

MIKA CHAMBA VALLEY

Located in the quiet and peaceful suburbs of Chamba valley away from the hustle and bustle of city life. It offers Four-star accommodation with its 78 modern fully furnished comfortable rooms. Our 54 standard double and twin rooms fitted with exquisite décor.
